Use this Railway Start Command:

sh start.sh

Do not manually wrap $PORT in quotes. Railway injects PORT automatically at runtime.
Do not use:
  gunicorn app:app --bind 0.0.0.0:'$PORT'
  gunicorn app:app --bind '0.0.0.0:$PORT'

Direct alternative if you do not want start.sh:
  gunicorn app:app --bind 0.0.0.0:$PORT --worker-class gevent --workers ${WEB_CONCURRENCY:-2} --worker-connections ${WEB_WORKER_CONNECTIONS:-1000} --timeout ${WEB_TIMEOUT:-75}
